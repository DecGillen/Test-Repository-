We were absolutely delighted to be asked to perform at the opening of Dropbox Dublin's new office! Having just opened their office in Park Place, Hatch Street, we helped them celebrate the occassion with some wonderful music and dancing. It might come as a surprising choice to have a group of Irish Dancers "welcome" in your beautiful new hardwood floors, but thankfully there was some building materials still on hand for us to batter!

In 2017 we had the pleasure of performing with "Azereire" as part of the St. Patrick's day charity ball in Azerbaijain. Funds raised from the event go towards a local orphanage, where we visited and shared music and dance with the children they help. This was a tremendous event with a good cause that we were honoured to have taken part in.

In 2016, as part of the centenary celebrations we performed in Liberty Hall. We were joined on the night be the Dublin Ukelele Orchestra and thoroughly enjoyed being part of the celebrations.
